No Poverty-SDG 1

SDG 1 is all about ending poverty everywhere. It focuses on getting rid of extreme poverty, which is living on less than $1.25 a day. The goal is to make sure everyone has access to essential resources, like housing, food, and healthcare, and can control their own property and land. It also pushes for better social safety nets and aims to boost economic growth so people can be more resilient against tough times. Basically, it’s about making sure everyone has a fair shot at a decent life.
